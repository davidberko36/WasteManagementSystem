WELCOME TO GREENLIFE

MORE ABOUT US

GreenLife is a waste management provider based in the Accra, proudly serving individuals and businesses in the Greater Accra Region with their domestic and industrial waste management needs, accepting waste of all kind.

Industrial Services

We offer comprehensive waste management solutions for industries of all sizes. Our services include:

  • Large-scale waste collection
  • Hazardous waste disposal
  • Recycling programs for businesses
  • Custom waste management plans

Domestic Services

We provide efficient waste management solutions for households. Our services include:

  • Regular waste collection
  • Recycling services
  • Green waste disposal
  • Bulky item removal

Our Mission

At GreenLife, our mission is to create a cleaner, healthier environment for the Greater Accra Region through innovative and sustainable waste management practices. We are committed to reducing environmental impact, promoting recycling, and educating our community about responsible waste disposal.
